Week 6: Cultural Blog

Stereotype Threat

Wikipedia defines Stereotype threat as the experience of anxiety in a situation in which a person has the potential to confirm a negative stereotype about his or her social group which can lead to hindering their ability to perform at their best.

Stereotyping is widely evident whether it be positive or negative. People tend to be stereotypical about a group's gender, race or religion. The anxiety the person feels because of this stereotype can lead to various insecurities and self doubt.

How can we reduce this stereotype of groups ? Well I guess whether we like it or not it will always be there, but with communication and awareness, getting to know the people, would go a long way helping us remove such boundaries.
