Dealing With Conflict


One of the most prominent conflicts I faced was when I got into an argument with one of my friends, to call him a friend would actually be a stretch more of an acquaintance, I met him through a mutual group of friends and we were playing a friendly game of facts and figures questionnaire and I was considerably doing better than this "friend", Now when I think about it I am pretty sure he did not like that he was losing and thats why he suddenly brought up a topic that I was quite sensitive with, and though the group knew and I am pretty sure that so did he that I was not comfortable with it, he insisted on keep talking and raising issues with it, finally I lost my temper and ended up arguing with him (which was what he was looking to do), eventually we both stormed off and everyone was left in a sour mood.

After passing of a few days, I came to realize that I shouldn't have reacted the way I did, and should've handled the situation better. I even tried to reach out to the person but the relation between us was never the same, and he avoided me since then.

My actions definitely made the conflict worse, instead of managing and properly addressing the issue, anger overcame me and I lost my cool.

If I could go back i definitely would've tried to reason with him and sort it out.

I learned that the main reason he started to lash out at me was not actually because he didn't like me or has some problem with me but the way I made him feel about himself and should've done something to address that. So I guess it all comes down to empathy and realizing how the other person is feeling.
